About RamaRaju Chess Academy

At RamaRaju Chess Academy, we believe in nurturing strategic minds and fostering a love for chess. Founded by Grandmaster RamaRaju, our academy provides world-class online chess education for players of all ages and skill levels. Our mission is to empower students with critical thinking, problem-solving skills, and the discipline essential for excelling in chess and in life.

We combine personalized coaching with interactive learning tools to create an engaging and effective online experience. Join our community of passionate chess players and discover the joy of continuous improvement.

Our Services

We offer a diverse range of online chess programs designed to meet every student's needs and aspirations.

Beginner's Fundamentals

Learn the basics of chess, piece movements, essential rules, and opening principles. Perfect for new players of all ages.

Intermediate Tactics & Strategy

Develop advanced tactical vision, strategic planning, and understanding of positional play. Elevate your game to the next level.

Advanced Masterclass

Deep dive into complex openings, intricate endgames, and advanced psychological aspects of competitive chess. For aspiring professionals.

Personalized Coaching

One-on-one sessions with our expert coaches to address specific weaknesses, refine strengths, and tailor a unique training plan.

Tournament Preparation

Specialized training for competitive play, including time management, opening repertoire building, and psychological resilience.

Online Group Classes

Engage with peers in interactive group sessions, fostering a collaborative learning environment and friendly competition.
